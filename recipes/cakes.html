The Ultimate Matilda Chocolate Cake

Description

Inspired by the legendary movie, this chocolate cake is incredibly rich, deep, and mind-blowingly moist. The secret to its soft texture is using buttermilk and hot coffee, which "blooms" the cocoa powder to make it taste insanely chocolatey without actually tasting like coffee at all.

It is covered in a thick, glossy, fudgy chocolate frosting that makes it look exactly like the giant cake from the film.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup Dutch-processed cocoa powder
  • 2 cups granulated white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup buttermilk (makes the cake super tender)
  • 3 large eggs (room temperature)
  • 1 cup strongly brewed hot coffee
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (177°C) and grease your cake pans with butter.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Make a well in the center and add the oil, eggs, vanilla, and buttermilk. Whisk until smooth.
  4. Slowly pour in the hot coffee, whisking gently. (The batter will look very thin and liquidy—that is normal!).
  5. Divide the batter evenly into your pans and bake for 25–30 minutes until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  6. Let the cakes cool completely before smothering them in chocolate fudge frosting.
